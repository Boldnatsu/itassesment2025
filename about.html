About the Company

At BIGWASH, we believe your vehicle deserves the best. Whether it’s two wheels or four, we specialize in delivering top-tier bike and car wash services that leave your ride looking spotless, shiny, and showroom-ready.

Founded with a passion for clean vehicles and happy customers, our mission is simple: provide fast, eco-friendly, and affordable wash services without compromising on quality. We use the latest technology, premium cleaning products, and a skilled team to make sure every wash is thorough, safe, and efficient.

From quick rinses to deep cleans, we cater to all types of vehicles with packages tailored to your needs. Whether you’re a daily commuter, a road trip enthusiast, or just someone who loves a clean ride, we’re here to help you drive with pride.

About the Founder

Bold Jung Poudel

Hi, I’m Bold Jung Poudel, the founder of BIGWASH, and a lifelong biking enthusiast with a passion for clean rides and quality service. What started as a simple weekend routine of detailing my own bike turned into a full-fledged idea to help others keep their rides looking and performing at their best.

I launched BIGWASH with a vision to bring professional, affordable, and eco-friendly bike wash services to riders who care about their machines as much as I do. With a background in mechanical work, customer service, and entrepreneurship, I’ve combined technical knowledge and a love for bikes to create a service that’s fast, reliable, and driven by detail.

For me, it’s not just about cleaning bikes—it’s about building a community of riders who value care, maintenance, and pride in ownership. Every service we offer reflects the same level of attention and dedication I’d give to my own ride.

Thanks for being part of the journey. Let’s keep your bike looking as good as the day you got it.
